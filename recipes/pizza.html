Pizza

Description

This beautiful pizza will fill your stomach to the brim with cheese and cheese!

For this recipe you need the following ingridients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Whatever topping you fancy
  • CHEESE!

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat the oven to a temperature a lot higher than room temperature
  2. Prepare your toppings
  3. Flatten the dough on a flat surface until its really flat
  4. Put on the tomato sauce
  5. Put the topping on the pizza
  6. CHEEEESEEE!
  7. Put the pizza in the oven
  8. Take the pizza out before it burns
